Quiz Rules & Regulations

General Rules

  • The quiz consists of multiple-choice questions.
  • Each question has only one correct answer.
  • You cannot go back to previous questions once submitted.
  • Total time allowed is 30 minutes.
  • Each correct answer awards 1 point.

Important Instructions

  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection.
  • Do not refresh the page during the quiz.
  • Answer all questions before the timer runs out.
  • Your progress will be auto-saved.
  • Results will be displayed immediately after completion.

Technical Requirements

  • Use a modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari).
  • Enable JavaScript in your browser.
  • Minimum screen resolution: 1024x768.
